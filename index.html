About me

I come from and live in Slovakia, Europe. I stuck around computers from childhood and ended up studying software engineering and working as a web developer. Programming allows me to solve people's problems through technology.

With my developer hat on, I look for simple, elegant, and practical solutions. "Done is better than perfect" is a phrase that guides me every day.

I got fascinated by startups a few years back and gathered much experience when trying to build two of my own. Most of my professional expertise also comes from startup companies, and that's the environment in which I feel energized and motivated.

In my free time, I spend offline time with my family and cats or read self-improvement and business books. I used to organize Startup Weekend Bratislava and mentor at Rails Girls events. I enjoy playing soccer with friends and swimming.
